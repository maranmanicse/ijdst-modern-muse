Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my research paper?

Visit our submission page and follow the online submission process. Make sure your manuscript follows our formatting guidelines.

What is the typical review timeline?

The peer review process typically takes 4-6 weeks. You'll receive regular updates about your submission status via email.

Are there any publication fees?

IJDST operates on an open-access model. Please contact us directly for information about article processing charges (APC).

How can I join the editorial board?

We welcome applications from qualified researchers. Please visit our editorial board page and submit your CV and cover letter.
