Why Join IJDST Editorial Board?

The International Journal for Development of Science & Technology (IJDST) is actively seeking qualified researchers and academics to join our editorial board. As an editorial board member, you will play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and integrity of published research.

Our editorial board comprises distinguished scholars from around the world who are committed to advancing scientific knowledge and fostering excellence in research publication.

Benefits of Joining

Academic Recognition

Enhance your academic profile and gain recognition in your field of expertise

Global Network

Connect with researchers and academics from around the world

Shape Research Quality

Contribute to maintaining high standards in scientific publishing

Early Access

Get early access to cutting-edge research in your field

Requirements & Qualifications

  • Ph.D. or equivalent doctorate degree in a relevant field
  • Established track record of peer-reviewed publications in reputable journals
  • Current affiliation with a recognized academic or research institution
  • Expertise in one or more areas relevant to the journal's scope
  • Commitment to ethical publishing practices and research integrity
  • Availability to review manuscripts and provide timely feedback
