Submission Guidelines

1

Original Research

All submissions must be original work not previously published or under consideration elsewhere.

2

Format Requirements

Papers should be submitted in MS Word format (.doc or .docx) following our template guidelines.

3

Length & Structure

Papers should be 6-15 pages including abstract, keywords, introduction, methodology, results, discussion, conclusion, and references.

4

Language

Papers must be written in clear, grammatically correct English.

5

Plagiarism Check

All papers will be checked for plagiarism. Papers with more than 20% similarity will be rejected.
