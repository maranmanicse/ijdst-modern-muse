FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How many days will it take for my paper to be published?
Once accepted and processing charges are completed, papers are typically published within 2–7 working days.
Do you provide a DOI for published papers?
Yes. Authors can request a DOI by emailing editor@ijdst.com after publication.
How many authors per paper are allowed?
Up to 13 co-authors are allowed per manuscript without extra charges. All authors receive individual e-certificates.
Is there a page limit for manuscripts?
No strict page limit. We recommend up to 30 pages for readability. No additional charges apply.
Does IJDST accept non-STEM submissions?
Yes — IJDST is multidisciplinary and accepts submissions across all major fields of study.
Are papers published in languages other than English?
Currently, IJDST publishes manuscripts in English only.
