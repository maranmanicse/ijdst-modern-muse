About IJDST

The International Journal for Development of Science & Technology (IJDST) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across multiple disciplines.

Our mission is to provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and practitioners to share their findings and contribute to the global body of scientific knowledge.
